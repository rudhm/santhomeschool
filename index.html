About The School

A Legacy of Integral Development

Santhome Higher Secondary School is more than just an educational institution; it is a community grounded in faith and dedicated to academic rigor. Managed by the Satna Diocesan Society in collaboration with the Sisters of The Daughters of St. Thomas.

We are committed to the integral development of our students, fostering intellectual growth alongside moral and spiritual maturity.
